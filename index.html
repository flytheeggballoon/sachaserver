  2. Click Multiplayer

  5. Click Done and join!

Bedrock Edition

  1. Open Minecraft Bedrock Edition
  2. Go to the Servers tab
  3. Click Add Server
  4. Enter IP: play.sachaserver.xyz
  5. Port: 19132 (default)
  6. Save and connect!
